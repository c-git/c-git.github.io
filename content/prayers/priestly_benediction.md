Source: Numbers Chapter 6: 22-27

> _22_ The Lord said to Moses, _23_ “Say to Aaron and his sons, Thus you shall bless the people of Israel: you shall say to them,
>
> ```text
> 24    The Lord bless you and keep you:
> 25    The Lord make his face to shine upon you, and be gracious to you:
> 26    The Lord lift up his countenance upon you, and give you peace.
> ```
>
> _27_ “So shall they put my name upon the people of Israel, and I will bless them.”

Regarded as a good prayer to pray over your children. Got it from [The Bible in a Year Podcast](https://bibleinayear.fireside.fm/day-57-2026) and tracked it down more easily because of the [transcript](https://podcasts.happyscribe.com/the-bible-in-a-year-with-fr-mike-schmitz/day-57-hear-o-israel-2026#t823-14) that came up when I searched.
